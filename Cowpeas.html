Cowpeas

Requirment Discription
Annual Rainfall Requiremnt 500 mm/year
Soil Preference Sandy topsoil with 5-10% clay and sandy loam and underneath with 10-2-% clay content.Needs 1,2 m deep soils.
Sowing depth 5-10 cm in sandy soils, 3-7 cm in heavy soils
Sowing Time Since cowpeas are cold-sensitive they should be planted early in the season.
Harvesting Harvest when the color starts to change from green to purplish or cream.
